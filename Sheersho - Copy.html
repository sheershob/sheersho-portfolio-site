Happiest day of my life

My happiest day occurred back in 2021, 6th January 2021 to be specific. It was a Saturday and it was three in the afternoon and I was getting ready for the chess club trials. I was nervous in the beginning but I brushed my feelings aside and tried my best. A blunder from the opponent and I won the first game. My confidence was sky high. At the end of the tournament I secured the third position winning six out of seven matches. That day got me really motivated me to play chess on a regular basis.

How the pandemic shaped me

The lockdown due to the pandemic started in March 2020 and it's been some time since that. There have been a ton of things that we've been through just by sitting in our homes. Most of the people were trying their hand out at cooking, some of us learnt coding, but as students we didn't have much choice but study for our boards and JEE. The uncertainty in the date of the exam was a test of our patience and for how long we could stretch on with the chaos around us. Some of us might have snapped and that is what made us stronger as a human because we have all survived it, and things are just going to get better from now on.
